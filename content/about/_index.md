# Namaskaram!

I am Krishna, an undergrad student of law at the Institute of Law, Nirma University. I am currently interested in pursuing academic research in the fields of constitutional law, cyber law and digital forensics. Apart from this, I find myself indulging in books, music and software development.

## Bluesky

In an attempt to reduce my digital footprint, I have drastically reduced my use of social media, opting to building this platform from scratch. Although, I cannot discount the benefit of these platforms in helping keep up to date with legal and political developments. You can find me on Bluesky [@krishnaj.space](https://bsky.app/profile/krishnaj.space)

## Github

Apart from legal studies, I have always been fascinated with computers and programming languages. I began learning Python when I was 8 and have since been passionate about software development. My feeble skirmishes are publicly available on Github [@krsnajani](https://github.com/krsnajani)

## Bandcamp

Music has always been an important part of who I am. I was fortunately exposed to the rich tradition to Hindustani Classical Music at a young age and have since been a disciple of the discipline. Once again I insist on keeping away from Big Tech platforms, opting to buy music rather than streaming it on Spotify. I maintain an ever increasing library of interesting lesser-known titles over on Bandcamp [@krsnaj](https://bandcamp.com/krsnaj)
