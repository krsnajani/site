About Me!

Haven't got a lot to say about myself because I am not so sure about what defines me yet. I think that introductions are 'self indulgent' and foreign as one is asked to observe themselves in third person. Its dissociative in that sense. Nonetheless, I am a student by profession (if you can call it that), a musician and a book collector (ref). I am currently undergoing an undergraduate program in law after which I intend to pursue a post-graduate and PhD in law and allied disciplines. I am what is called an aspiring academic. Whether this is a socially ordained title that I have internalised or a real genuine interest? I cant figure out yet. I am scared that doing so would rat me out as an imposter.

If you did go through the stream-of-consciousness gibberish above, here is a short list of places around the internet where you can find me

    • I sure do hope to also reach a point in life where like Dr. Knuth I can say:

    I have been a happy man ever since January 1, 1990, when I no longer had an email address. I'd used email since about 1975, and it seems to me that 15 years of email is plenty for one lifetime.
